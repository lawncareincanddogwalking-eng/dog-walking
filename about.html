About My Services

Premium Neighborhood Walks

I provide focused, one-hour walking sessions designed to give your dog the perfect amount of exercise and mental stimulation. We explore local neighborhood paths together, ensuring your pup stays active and happy. Once our hour of adventure is complete, I personally drop your dog back off at your home, safe and sound.

Reliable Dog Sitting

In addition to walking, I offer dedicated dog sitting services for when you're away or busy. I’ll provide companionship, playtime, and regular care within the comfort of your home. As long as food and necessary supplies are provided, I’ll ensure your dog is fed on schedule and perfectly cared for.
