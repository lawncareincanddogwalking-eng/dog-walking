About My Service

The Perfect Walk

I provide focused, one-hour walks throughout the neighborhood, ensuring your dog gets plenty of fresh air and exercise. Every session is an adventure! After our hour of exploration, I personally drop your pup back off at your home, making sure they are safe, hydrated, and happy.

Professional Dog Sitting

Beyond walking, I offer dedicated dog sitting services. Whether you are away for the day or just need extra help, I am available to provide companionship and care. If food and supplies are provided, I will handle all feedings and routine care to keep your dog's schedule exactly as it should be.
